Info feed:


Although this component isn't actually used as a blog feed on this site, I though this, or a similar concept, would make an awesome way to display a blog archive to users. By switching out some of the imgs on the line with link to different blog post or imgs, I think this would be a fun way to make a blog feed.

Visualization:

This particular component consists of multiple [div] sections, with [img] to the left or the right signifying a new section. Each section has a brief [p] section describing it.
